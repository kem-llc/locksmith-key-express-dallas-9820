Common questions

Should I rekey or replace my locks?

Rekeying is cheaper if your locks are in good condition and you just want new keys. Replace if locks are old or damaged.

Can you install smart locks on any door?

Most standard doors can accommodate smart locks. We’ll assess your door thickness and alignment to ensure compatibility.
