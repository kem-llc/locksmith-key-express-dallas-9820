Our story

Your Trusted Dallas Locksmith

To provide fast, reliable, and affordable locksmith services to the Dallas community with integrity and expertise.

Locksmith Key Express was founded in Dallas with a simple mission: deliver prompt, professional locksmith service without the inflated prices. Our founder, a seasoned locksmith with over 15 years of experience, saw a need for a trustworthy company that puts customers first. Starting with a single van, we’ve grown to a fleet serving the entire Dallas metroplex.

Today, we’re proud to be a locally owned and operated business that treats every customer like a neighbor. Our technicians undergo rigorous training and background checks, ensuring you get both skill and peace of mind. From emergency lockouts to comprehensive security upgrades, we’re here for you 24/7.
