Our story

Your Trusted Dallas Locksmith

To provide fast, transparent, and professional locksmith services that keep Dallas residents and businesses safe and secure.

Locksmith Key Express was founded with a simple mission: bring honesty and speed back to the locksmith industry. Our founder, a Dallas native, saw too many customers overcharged and left waiting for hours. We set out to change that by building a team of skilled, background-checked technicians who arrive fully equipped and give upfront pricing.

Today, we serve the entire Dallas metro area with 24/7 emergency service, residential and commercial lock solutions, and automotive key programming. We’re proud of our 5.0 rating and the trust our community places in us. Every job, big or small, gets the same commitment to quality and reliability.
